How did the universe become biggger and bigger?

According to modern science, the universe began about 13.8 billion years ago with the Big Bang, a moment when space itself began expanding. Since then, it's grown into the vast, dynamic cosmos we observe today. It has no known edge, no confirmed center, and possibly no end. Just galaxies upon galaxies, all dancing to the laws of physics.
