How did the universe become biggger and bigger?


According to modern science, the universe began about 13.8 billion years ago with the Big Bang, a moment when space itself began expanding. Since then, it's grown into the vast, dynamic cosmos we observe today. It has no known edge, no confirmed center, and possibly no end. Just galaxies upon galaxies, all dancing to the laws of physics.


Types of Universe:



Open Universe:

Continues expanding forever as gravity isn't strong enough to slow it down.

When the avg density of the universe is greater tha its critical density it forms Open Universe.


Closed Universe:

Eventually collapses in a "Big Crunch" due to strong gravitational pull.

When the avg density of the universe is less than its critical density than it forms Closed Universe.


Flat Universe

Expands at a steady rate due to a perfect balance between gravity and dark energy.

When the avg and critical density of the universe are both equal it forms Flat Universe.


Multiverse Theory

Suggests multiple universes exist with different physical laws and properties.
